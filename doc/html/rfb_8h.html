◆ UPDATE_BUF_SIZE

#define UPDATE_BUF_SIZE   32768

UPDATE_BUF_SIZE must be big enough to send at least one whole line of the framebuffer.

So for a max screen width of say 2K with 32-bit pixels this means 8K minimum.
